Exercising is crucial for staying healthy, but many disabled children are not able to exercise enough. The Taipei Swimming Association for the Disabled hopes to make the disabled children healthier through providing free swimming lessons.

Swimming could strengthen muscles, increase flexibility and increase lung capacity, which is very beneficial to the body. There are many passionate volunteer coaches in this association. Coach Chen Lunzhong, for example, has more than twenty years of experience in coaching disabled people. Not only will disabled people learn how to swim by participating in our programs, but they also will learn how to get dressed, bathe, use the toilet, and follow the rules.

The swimming lessons are provided for the disabled every Monday to Saturday. Anyone with the Disability Card may join the association and receive free training. If you wish to become one of the volunteer coaches, please call the phone number below.
