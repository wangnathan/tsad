Who We Serve

We serve those struggling with physical disabilities, mental disabilities, learning disorders, visual impairment, mood disorders, etc. Anyone with a Disability Card can join us and receive free swimming lessons. For more information about the Disability Card, visit this web page: http://disable.yam.org.tw/about-din
